Project Overview

A modern, user-friendly logistics and courier management system specifically tailored for Airpack Express operations. Built with Python and Tkinter, it provides a fast and reliable native desktop experience without requiring an active internet connection.

It seamlessly handles complex daily operations such as secure billing, comprehensive consignment management, robust master data storage, and complete invoice generation, ensuring all shipment records and financial calculations are accurately maintained in the generated logistics reports.

Key Features

Intuitive Billing & Invoicing
Create, print, and manage localized logistics bills with automatic number generation and accurate data calculations.
Consignment Management
Track and record detailed shipment data, ensuring structured handling of every package from entry to delivery.
Master Data Storage
Securely store and maintain customer profiles and sender/receiver details for quick access during new bill creation.
Comprehensive Reports & Checking
View, search, and verify past operations, effortlessly tracking history and auditing older bills with ease.
Clean Application Dashboard
With a simple, distraction-free Tkinter user interface mapped natively to SQLite for reliable, instant data updates.
