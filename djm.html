A modern, user-friendly billing and inventory management system for jewellery shops.

Preview Gallery

Project Overview

A modern, user-friendly billing and inventory management system specifically tailored for jewellery shops. Built with Python and Tkinter, it provides a fast and reliable native desktop experience without requiring an active internet connection.

It seamlessly handles complex daily operations such as secure billing, ongoing stock management, new purchases processing, dedicated customer schemes, and complete GST-compliant invoice generation ensuring all legal and tax requirements are strictly met in generated reports.

Key Features

Intuitive Billing
Create, print, and manage bills with GST, old gold/silver, and multiple payment modes.
Stock Management
Track finished and unfinished items, auto-update stock instantly on billing and purchase.
Purchases
Record new stock purchases and accurately update semi-finished stock availability.
Comprehensive Dashboard
View, search, and securely print past bills alongside a detailed monthly sales summary.
Monthly Customer Scheme
Efficiently manage specialized scheme customers and closely track their recurring payments.
Rates & Taxes
Instantly update daily gold/silver market rates and adjusting GST percentages.
Automatic Backups
Secure weekly database backup routines automatically writing directly to C://Backup (Windows).
Automatic PDF Cleanup
System hygiene tasks ensuring old generated bill PDFs are deleted monthly to save storage.
Robust Invoice Regeneration
Strictly accurate invoices can be completely regenerated from the database at any moment in time.
